Conference paper: Collision Avoidance Techniques in UAV's

Abstract

h3>. 

  This paper is a comprehensive comparison of collision avoidance techniques for Unmanned Aerial Vehicles researching the most effective techniques while taking into account the assets and liabilities presented by their usage in recent years by the military or other unaffiliated groups. This is an important field of study due to the predicted rise of the use of UAVs in the commercial industry as said by the department for science and technology in order to offer new capabilities, increase productivity, lower emissions, and minimize danger to life while safely and fairly sharing airspace with other users in order to benefit the economy and larger society.
